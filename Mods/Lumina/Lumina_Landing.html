Lumina

Enhance your gaming experience with Lumina's stunning visual effects and tonemapping features, designed to bring your game world to life. With custom LUTs, advanced tonemapping techniques, and HDR support, Lumina enhances every scene, making your gaming experience more immersive and visually stunning.

While Lumina is available for free on Paradox Mods, your generous donations help us continue to improve and expand its features. Please note that when donating, you must link your email to ensure that we can send you exclusive updates, early access features, and personalized support. Your contribution, no matter how big or small, supports the ongoing development of Lumina and keeps the project alive.

Cities Skylines II Required

Lumina is designed for use with Cities Skylines II. To get the most out of Lumina's advanced visual effects and tonemapping features, make sure you have the game installed and updated to the latest version.

Installation Instructions:

  • Purchase Lumina directly from this page.
  • After your purchase, the mod's files will be emailed to you.
  • Download the Lumina mod from your email.
  • Unzip the downloaded file into your Mods folder located in your game directory.
  • Ensure that the Lumina folder is placed in ./Mods/Lumina/.
  • Launch Cities Skylines II and enable Lumina in the mod settings.
  • For more detailed steps, visit the Lumina Documentation.
  • Current version: 1.7 Sienna Development Build
