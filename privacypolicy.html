Google AdSense is an advertising service provided by Google that allows us to display advertisements on our website. These ads are tailored to your interests and may be based on your browsing history.

Google AdSense may collect the following information when you visit our website:

Why We Use Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to help support our website and provide you with valuable content and services for free. The revenue generated from these ads contributes to the maintenance and development of our site.

Your Ad Personalization Choices

Google AdSense allows you to control your ad personalization preferences. You can adjust your preferences by visiting Google's Ad Settings. This enables you to manage the types of ads you see and the data used to display them.
